Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

At Dynamic Overseas Services Pvt. Ltd., we prioritize the privacy of our visitors and clients. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of personal information collected, processed, and stored by our platform.

1. Information We Collect

When you register on our website, fill out contact forms, or purchase a service, we may collect the following details:

  • Personal identifiers (Full Name, Email Address, Phone Number).
  • Professional details (Current CV, educational background, employment history, and targeted countries).
  • Technical data (IP Address, browser type, cookies, and page visit metrics).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following professional purposes:

  • To prepare, format, and optimize your professional CV and Cover Letter.
  • To assist with manual or automated job application portal entries.
  • To correspond with you regarding drafts, feedback, and support inquiries.
  • To process billing transactions for selected packages.

3. Data Protection and Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your personal details. We do not sell, trade, or share your resume content or contact files with unauthorized third parties. Access to your details is strictly limited to our writers and profile coordinators handling your order.

4. Cookies and Web Analytics

Our website utilizes cookies to analyze traffic patterns, remember user inputs, and optimize page speeds. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser options, though some interactive elements may not load optimally.

5. Updates to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be updated on this page with an updated modification date.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy guidelines or data retention policies, please email us at info@dynamicoverseas.com.
