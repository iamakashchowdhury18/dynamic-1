Refund & Cancellation Policy

Effective Date: 29 June 2026

Dynamic Overseas Services

Effective Date: 29 June 2026

1. Overview

This Refund & Cancellation Policy applies to all services offered by Dynamic Overseas Services through dynamicservice.in.

By placing an order or making payment, you agree to this Policy.

2. Nature of Services

Our services include professional:

  • CV / Resume Writing
  • Cover Letter Writing
  • Resume Formatting
  • Basic Job Application Assistance (where applicable)

These are personalized digital services created specifically for each client.

3. Cancellation Policy

Clients may request cancellation within one (1) hour of successful payment, provided that work on the order has not substantially commenced.

Cancellation requests must be sent to:

Email: help@dynamicservice.in

The email should include:

  • Full Name
  • Registered Email Address
  • Mobile Number
  • Payment Reference / Transaction ID
  • Reason for Cancellation

4. Refund Policy

Eligible Refund

A full refund may be provided where:

  • Cancellation is requested within one (1) hour of successful payment; and
  • Work has not substantially commenced.

Non-Refundable Situations

Except where required by applicable law, refunds will generally not be available after:

  • One (1) hour has passed since successful payment; or
  • Work has substantially commenced; or
  • The CV, Resume, or Cover Letter has been delivered; or
  • Job application assistance has been completed.

Because our services involve personalized digital work, the value of the service lies in the time, expertise, and effort invested in preparing the documents.

5. Service Quality

If a client believes there is an error in the delivered document due to our mistake (for example, a formatting issue or information entered incorrectly despite being correctly provided by the client), we will make reasonable corrections at no additional cost.

This correction process is separate from the refund policy.

6. No Guarantee of Employment or Visa

Payment is made for professional document preparation and related services only.

Dynamic Overseas Services does not guarantee:

  • Job offers
  • Interview calls
  • Employment
  • Salary level
  • Work permit
  • Visa approval
  • Immigration approval

Refunds will not be granted solely because the client does not receive interviews, employment, or a visa.

7. Refund Processing

If a refund is approved:

  • It will generally be processed to the original payment method.
  • Processing times may vary depending on the payment provider or bank.

8. Fraud Prevention

Dynamic Overseas Services reserves the right to refuse refund requests where there is evidence of:

  • Fraudulent payment activity
  • Chargeback abuse
  • False claims
  • Misuse of services
  • Violation of our Terms & Conditions

This does not affect any rights available under applicable law.
