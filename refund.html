Refund Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

Thank you for choosing Dynamic Overseas Services Pvt. Ltd. We strive to provide premium career preparation documents and support services. Please read our refund guidelines below before initiating payments.

1. General Policy

Because our services involve customized document writing, research, and individual consultant allocations, **service fees are generally non-refundable once work has started**.

2. Before Work Commences

If you have paid for a service package but wish to cancel *before* any action has been taken on our end (e.g., prior to scheduling an intake discussion, starting a resume analysis, or drafting any documents), you are eligible for a **full refund minus a 5% administrative payment processing fee**.

All cancellation requests must be submitted in writing via email to info@dynamicservice.in within 24 hours of package purchase.

3. After Work Commences

Once our writers have initiated your profile review, conducted a consultations meeting, or delivered a first draft, the purchase is **strictly non-refundable**. This policy compensates for the highly customized research time, drafting resources, and direct labor committed to your career assets.

4. Revisions and Satisfaction

We are fully committed to your satisfaction. All packages include a review and revision cycle. If you feel changes are needed on your draft, you can request revisions within the standard review timelines, and our writers will edit and adjust the documents accordingly.

5. Contact for Refund Inquiries

If you need clarification on your order status or wish to request cancellation, please contact our billing coordinators directly at info@dynamicservice.in.
